# Privacy Policy for VoiceBudget

**Last Updated:** January 16, 2026

## Introduction

VoiceBudget ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Account Information:** Email address and password when you create an account
- **Expense Data:** Transaction amounts, descriptions, categories, and dates that you input
- **Voice Recordings:** Audio recordings when you use the voice input feature

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system version
- **Usage Data:** App features used, interaction patterns (anonymized)

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- Provide and maintain the VoiceBudget service
- Process your voice recordings to extract expense information
- Display your expense history and analytics
- Improve our AI parsing accuracy
- Send important service notifications

## Voice Recording Processing

When you record a voice memo:
1. The audio is sent securely to our AI processing service
2. The speech is converted to text and parsed for expense details
3. **Voice recordings are NOT stored permanently** - they are deleted immediately after processing
4. Only the extracted text (transcript) and expense data are saved to your account

## Data Storage and Security

- Your expense data is stored securely on Supabase (hosted on AWS)
- All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
- We implement industry-standard security measures
- Your data is associated with your account and not shared with other users

## Third-Party Services

VoiceBudget uses the following third-party services:
- **Supabase:** Database and authentication (Privacy Policy: https://supabase.com/privacy)
- **OpenAI Whisper API:** Speech-to-text processing (Privacy Policy: https://openai.com/privacy)
- **OpenAI GPT:** Expense parsing (Privacy Policy: https://openai.com/privacy)

## Data Retention

- **Account Data:** Retained while your account is active
- **Expense Data:** Retained until you delete it or close your account
- **Voice Recordings:** Deleted immediately after processing (not stored)

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access:** Request a copy of your data
- **Correction:** Update or correct your information
- **Deletion:** Delete your account and all associated data
- **Export:** Export your expense data

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@voicebudget.app

## Children's Privacy

VoiceBudget is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date.

## Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
- Email: privacy@voicebudget.app
- Website: https://voicebudget.app/contact
